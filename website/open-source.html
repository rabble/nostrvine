üôè Special Thanks to ArchiveTeam

We owe a huge debt of gratitude to ArchiveTeam for their dedicated volunteer work in 2017 to preserve all the original Vines and metadata when Twitter announced Vine's shutdown.

Without their amazing effort to archive over 900 million Vines before the platform went offline, it wouldn't be possible to restore and celebrate these creative moments. Their commitment to digital preservation ensures that internet culture and creativity live on.

"ArchiveTeam's mission is to save our digital heritage. We are a loose collective of rogue archivists, programmers, writers and loudmouths dedicated to saving our digital heritage."
